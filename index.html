WELCOME TO COMMUNITY CROWDSOURCING WEBSITE

Community Crowdsourcing website allow rural communities to crowdsource their traditional objects to be modeled into 3D formats by graphic designers on the global net.

One of the ethnical groups in Namibia actively using indigenous Knowledge for survival is the ovaHimba community. They reside in the Kunene region in the North of Namibia, and their main way of survival is by exercising their acquired experience of the land, which they have gained through an active engagement with nature and its properties through time.
